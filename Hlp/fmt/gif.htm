.GIF - Compuserve Graphics interchange format

Features:

  • Pixel formats: 1..8 bits/pixel. Uninterlaced and interlaced.

  • Compression: LZW.

  • Transparency: Transparent color.

  • Meta info: Comment, Adobe XMP (Name, Author, Comment, Copyright, Keywords, Density, Creation date, GPS location).

  • Notes: Both v87a and v89a are supported. Animated GIF's are also supported (disposal methods 0, 1, 2 only). Overlay text (if present) is ignored.
